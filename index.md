# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use our application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to create timelapse videos from your photos locally on your device. To provide this functionality, we require certain permissions, but **we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.**

### a. Device Permissions
To function correctly, the App requests the following permissions:
*   **Camera:** To allow you to take photos directly within the App.
*   **Photo Library / External Storage:** To allow you to select existing photos to create timelapses and to save the generated videos to your device's gallery.

### b. Facial Recognition Technology
The App utilizes on-device machine learning (e.g., Google ML Kit) to detect faces in your photos. This is purely for feature functionality (such as keeping faces aligned during the timelapse).
*   **Local Processing:** All face detection processing is done locally on your device.
*   **No Data Transmission:** We do not send, share, or store facial data, images, or metadata to our servers or any third-party clouds.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Any data accessed by the App (photos, videos, facial landmarks) is used exclusively on your device to:
*   Generate the timelapse video sequences.
*   Align and stabilize images based on local face tracking.
*   Save the final video locally to your device’s gallery.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Since the App processes everything locally, we do not share your photos or personal data with third-party tracking or advertising services. However, the App is distributed via the Google Play Store, which may collect basic app usage metrics and crash reports according to Google's own Privacy Policy.

## 4. Data Retention

Because the App does not transmit data to our servers, we do not have a data retention period. Your photos and created videos remain on your device and are subject to your own device’s security and storage management.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not intentionally collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of our App collecting personal data from minors.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
